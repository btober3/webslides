LIBRARY RESOURCES

The Library Resources Program supports Utah's state agencies and their employees and
promotes access to library resources through interlibrary loan, government publications
online resources, reference services, and more.

Library Card

Register your card

• Click on Log In (Online Catalog right-hand corner)
• Your username is the 14 digit barcode #
• The last 4 digits of the barcode is your password

UGDL

Permanent access to born digital and digitized publications created by State of Utah agencies.

  • Central depository for state digital publications

    Currently houses over 87,000 items

  • Ease of access

    Browse state agency publications in one location

  • Copyright free

    Majority of items are in the public domain

Interlibrary Loan

Borrow materials from other libraries.

  • Work-related request

    email the title, author, journal title, and article title of your request along with any other pertinent information.

  • Extended checkout

    Six weeks if there are no other requests for the item.

  • Interlibrary Loan email

    ill@utah.gov

Professional development resources

  • Identify a title that would be helpful for you in your work
  • Search the library catalog for the title
  • If it is currently held by the USL, click on “Place a Request”
  • If it is not currently held by the USL, email our acquisitions staff at libraryacquisitions@utah.gov and request that the title be purchased
