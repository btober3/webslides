Search

To get the most out of your search combine all 3 of the following search strategies (not an option with every resource).

  • Boolean

    AND: Each result contains all search terms.

    OR: Each result contains at least one search term.

    NOT: None of the results contain the specified term.

  • Wildcards and Truncation

    # Hashtag

    * Asterisk

    ? Question mark

    The asterisk (*) wildcard is also known as the truncation wildcard.

  • Narrow your results

    Facets: Subject terms, Publisher, Geography, Language

    Limiters: Full text, Peer Reviewed, Publication date

    Source types: Academic Journals, Magazines, Reviews, Newspapers
